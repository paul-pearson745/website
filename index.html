How to prevent obesity in children.

There are many ways to prevent chilhood obesity and some of these ways may seem simpe but can also be really challenging for the children.       

1. Doing exercise 

Many children don't like to do exercise because they would rather stay indoors on computers. For some children, they enjoy going outdoors and playing sport which is helping them to lose weight. So if everybody can motivate young children to do exercise then the obesity levels might decline.               

2. Following correct diet 

All children must follow the correct diet in order to stay a healthy weight. This diet must consist of 5 fruit or vegetables a day, 3 meals a day (breakfast, lunch, dinner) and also 8 glasses of fluis a day. This will mean that you are following the correct diet and will be able to maintain a healthy weight.               

3. Correct hours of sleep 

The correct hours of sleep can make a massive impact on the human body. For children 6-13 years old , 9-11 hours of sleep is recommended. This means that after the recommended hours of sleep, you are to wake up and do exercise so that the the excess fat is being used up.

4. Checking BMI

When checking BMI, this will tell you a rough idea on whether the child is obese or even underweight. This will mean that if the child is said to be overweight without muscle (pure fat) then they will be able to do exercise and follow a strict diet to help them lose the fat.
